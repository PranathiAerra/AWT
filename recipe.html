OUR RECIPES

Hyderabad Biryani

  • Marinate Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, spices, lemon juice, and half the fried onions. Marinate for 2 hours or overnight.
  • Cook Rice: Boil water with salt and whole spices. Add soaked rice and cook until 70% done. Drain.
  • Layering: In a heavy pot, spread marinated chicken. Top with mint, coriander, and half the rice. Repeat layers. Finish with saffron milk, ghee, and fried onions.
  • Dum Cooking: Cover tightly with foil and lid. Cook on high for 5 mins, then low for 20 mins. Rest for 10 mins before serving.

Indian Thali

  • Cook Dal & Rice: Pressure cook tur dal and rice separately.
  • Sambar: Boil drumsticks and pumpkin. Sauté mustard, chana dal, methi, red chilies, and hing. Add shallots, tomato, tamarind water, spices, and cooked dal. Simmer.
  • Rasam: Temper mustard, urad dal, red chilies, and curry leaves. Add tomato, tamarind water, rasam powder, and cooked dal. Boil and garnish with coriander.
  • Puri-Bhaji: Sauté mustard, urad dal, green chilies, and onions. Add mashed potatoes, turmeric, salt, and water. Cook and finish with lemon juice.
  • Appadam: Fry or roast papads.
  • Assemble: Serve everything hot with curd and pickle on the side.

Pulao

  • Sauté Spices: Heat oil/ghee in a pan. Add whole spices and let them sizzle.
  • Add Veggies: Add onions, sauté till golden. Add ginger-garlic paste, chilies, and tomatoes. Cook till soft.
  • Mix Rice & Veggies: Add chopped vegetables and drained rice. Sauté for 2 minutes.
  • Cook: Add water and salt. Cover and simmer on low heat until rice is cooked and fluffy (about 15–20 mins).
  • Rest & Serve: Let it rest for 5 mins. Fluff with a fork and serve hot.
